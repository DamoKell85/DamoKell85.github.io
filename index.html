What do you need to know about Damien?

My interest in computers began when I was eight years old and first played Minesweeper and Mahjong on a Windows 98 operating system. From there, my interests in mathematical logic and reasoning ability began to develop. I developed an interest in problem solving, be it a simple crossword or a Rubik's cube, or more complex problems such as math homework from school. That grounding in math and problem solving led me to explore the world of engineering. I now have three degrees all based in the area of engineering. I now spend my free time learning new programming languages and exploring new technologies
